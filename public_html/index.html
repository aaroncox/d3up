D3Up.com Retired

I've decided it's time to retire the servers hosting D3Up.com since it hasn't been updated for RoS. I posted a few months ago about my lack of time and open sourced the code to the site in hopes others can learn and build useful things with it. It's been coming to this for a while now and it's finally time to wind it down.

It's been an amazing ride and I'd like to thank the community for the wild ride we had building this amazing tool over the years. From everyone who emailed me with ideas, reported bugs, or those of you that helped me figure out some wacky math - thank you. I'd also like to thank Blizzard for making such a great game and being so friendly to their fansites! It was great to get to know members of the community and be a part of something like this.

If you need to reach out to me for some reason, you can always email me here. With that all being said, I'll see you all in Diablo!

If you're interested in some other great D3 resources I'd recommend:

  • /r/diablo - Reddit's Diablo Community
  • d3rawr - Another great calculator
  • Diablo Somepage - Good way to keep up on diablo news
